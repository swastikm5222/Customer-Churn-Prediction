# Business Understanding

## What is Customer Churn?

Customer churn refers to customers discontinuing a company's service or subscription.

In this dataset:

- Churn = Yes → Customer left the company
- Churn = No → Customer stayed

## Why Churn Matters

Acquiring a new customer is often more expensive than retaining an existing one.

High churn can result in:

- Revenue loss
- Reduced customer lifetime value
- Increased marketing costs
- Lower business growth

## Business Objective

Predict customers who are likely to churn before they leave.

This enables the company to:

- Offer targeted discounts
- Improve customer support
- Recommend better plans
- Increase retention

## How Machine Learning Helps

Machine learning can identify hidden patterns in customer behavior and predict churn risk.

Benefits:

- Early detection of high-risk customers
- Better customer retention
- Data-driven business decisions
- Increased profitability
